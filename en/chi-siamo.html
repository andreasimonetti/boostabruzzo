Conceived in 2018 by Fabrizio, BoostAbruzzo is an association of professionals from Abruzzo who live and work abroad but keep a special bond with their land. In January 2020, Fabrizio met Alessia; during the lockdown months they began recruiting brilliant minds to build the project: among them were Emilia and Andrea. This is how BoostAbruzzo was born, an association where members and mentors want to create a network to share experiences and knowledge with Abruzzo, participating - albeit from afar - in the enhancement of their land. 

During the studies and discussions on the analysis of the Abruzzo context, it emerged that although the region has invaluable resources and human capital, there is still a long way to go to achieve economic growth and sustainable development goals. BoostAbruzzo brings together professionals from Abruzzo who want to take part in the economic and cultural revival of the region from abroad and overturn the idea that people from Abruzzo abroad have of their own region. Abruzzo is not only a land of traditions and a fantastic place to spend a holiday, but also a fertile land in which to invest, to make creativity and innovation blossom. To do this, it is necessary to strengthen its roots and to invest in people.
