Conceived in 2018 by Fabrizio, BoostAbruzzo is an association of professionals from Abruzzo who live and work abroad but keep a special bond with their land. In January 2020, Fabrizio met Alessia; during the lockdown months they began recruiting brilliant minds to build the project: among them were Emilia and Andrea. This is how BoostAbruzzo was born, an association where members and mentors want to create a network to share experiences and knowledge with Abruzzo, participating - albeit from afar - in the enhancement of their land.

Read more
Mission

To change the belief of Abruzzo and its people through the mobilisation of the region’s human capital in the world, the boosting of Abruzzo start-ups and entrepreneurs and the promotion of creativity, innovation and entrepreneurship in the Abruzzo economy.

Vision

We believe in a dynamic and innovative Abruzzo where young entrepreneurs can grow and contribute to the sustainable development of its economy. The gentian, the symbol of BoostAbruzzo, represents creativity and innovation born from the rock.

Join BoostMe!

BoostMe is our kick-off project, a mentorship programme designed for anyone wishing to achieve business growth.
It is a dedicated initiative offering training and individual counselling with mentors from Abruzzo who gained their professional experience by moving abroad and exporting their regional identity. 

HOW TO JOIN

If you would like to expand your business, break into international markets, or develop an innovative product, you can sign up for the programme at no cost. You will find a team of professionals eager to meet emerging businesses from Abruzzo and share their knowledge, and who will guide you through defining your corporate identity.
